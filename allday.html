All-Day Sports Camps

Exclusive Locations! Please check out your location under the ATA Locations tab to ensure that the program is offered at your location.

The Camp is organized by a USPTA Certified Tennis Professional and will include 3-5 hours of on court tennis along with other sports such as; soccer, ball hockey, basketball, capture the flag, soccer baseball, touch football and more!! The camp will run rain or shine and will include a free BBQ lunch each Friday and healthy snacks throughout the week. Campers are encouraged to bring a bathing suit and towels on hot days to run through the water park next to the club. There is a TUCK Shop at the clubhouse where campers can purchase small snacks (such as freezies) during breaks and each camper should pack a lunch and snacks. (Racquets can be provided if needed).
