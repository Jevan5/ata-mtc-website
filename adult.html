Adult Beginner Series

This program is inteneded to provide introductory instruction to the 5 essential strokes in tennis: forehand, backhand, serve, volley and overhead. Participants will also be provided a basic understanding of the rules associated with singles and doubles play. Although technical analysis will be the primary focus, all lessons will remain active and fun.

Adult Refresher Series

This program will build upon stroke technique by introducing rally situations and directional play. The level will include an in-depth analysis of the rules and strategy of singles and doubles play. Participants will also be introduced to a standard warm-up routine. Information will be provided on “how to best utilize an hour of tennis”.
