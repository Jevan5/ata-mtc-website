Kanata Klassic

The Kanata Klassic is an open tennis tournament for Ottawa area residents hosted at the March Tennis Club. Founded by Jonathan Adamson in 2005, the Kanata Klassic has become one of the biggest tournaments in the city.

In recent years, ATA along with the March Tennis Club have joined forces with the Canadian Cancer Society and a portion of the proceeds from the Kanata Klassic are donated to the Canadian Cancer Society and its quest to find a cure for cancer.

We would like to thank all participants; past; present; and future; for your support. We hope that you have an enjoyable experience in this tournament. We would like to thank Ted Thompson along with YONEX and ASICS for their continued support of the Kanata Klassic.
